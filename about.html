About Us

At Agreement Drafts, we understand the importance of having well-crafted legal documents that meet your needs. Our innovative platform allows users to generate various legal agreements (registered or non-registered) with just a few clicks. We are dedicated to providing a user-friendly experience, ensuring that you can prepare documents in multiple languages.

Whether you're an individual or a business, our service is designed to simplify the process of creating essential agreements. Experience the convenience of tailored legal solutions with Agreement Drafts.

Our Services

  • Pay and download drafts only.
  • Submit draft that doesn't require registration (Services Offered: Payment of Stamp Duty + Notarization + Delivery).
  • Submit draft that requires Registration (Services Offered: Payment of Stamp Duty + Registration Fees + Photographs + Other formalities + Assistance at District Registrar Office).

Delivery Timeline

  • Unregistered agreements: Within 1-2* Hours.
  • Registered agreements: Within 0-2* Days.
